Issue 171130.1: Typo - Regression since DWARF3 standard

Author: Ben Dunbobbin
Champion: Ron Brender
Date submitted: 2017-11-30
Type: Editorial
Status: Accepted
DWARF Version: 6

Section E.3.2, pg 388

dwarf 5 text reads:

References to other .debug_infosections follow the approach suggested above, but the naming rule is slightly different in that the <file-designator> should be interpreted as a <file-designator>.

It should read the same as in the dwarf4 and dwarf3 spec:

References to other .debug_info sections follow the approach suggested above, but the naming rule might be slightly different in that the <file-designator> should be interpreted as a <function-designator>.

Probably just a simple copy and paste error led to <file-designator> being repeated twice.

RESOLUTION

This is a typo as surmised in the Issue report.

In line 24, replace "<file-designator>" with "<function-designator>".

02/22/21 -- Accepted
